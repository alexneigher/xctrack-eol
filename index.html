Thank You for 12 Incredible Years

XCTrack.me is being sunset after over a decade of service to the free flying community.

After 12 amazing years of operation, we regret to announce that XCTrack.me is being officially sunset. Rising infrastructure and maintenance costs have made continued operation unsustainable.

We're deeply grateful to all the pilots, organizers, and enthusiasts who trusted XCTrack.me to track their flights and adventures. It has been an honor to serve the community.
